Key Finding

The median founder would be richer selling at the IPO. But the mean Never-Sell Value exceeds the mean IPO Value — a small number of superstar founders who held concentrated positions in outperforming firms built extraordinary wealth. Superstar founders are built on superstar firms.

About This Research

“Superstar Founders” by Andrea Eisfeldt, Aria Liu & Yu Shi

Hand-collected dataset of 2,424 founders from 2,131 true IPOs (1996–2024).
Data sources: SEC Form 424(b) prospectuses, proxy statements (DEF 14A), Form 4 filings, CRSP, Compustat.
